================================================================================

DATA FLOW:
  User clicks Office tab
      ↓
  showTab('office') function
      ↓
  Display #tab-office div
      ↓
  Iframe loads /office endpoint
      ↓
  Flask serves pixel_office.html
      ↓
  Canvas renders agents
